Teriyaki Glaze

Adam Liaw

http://adamliaw.com/recipe/homemade-teriyaki-sauce/

Ingredients:

  • 250mL Soy Sauce
  • 200mL Mirin
  • 200mL Sake
  • 60-90g Sugar

Method:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan and place over medium heat, stirring until sugar dissolves. (Do not Boil)
  2. Allow to cool then transfer into glass bottle.
