American Tollhouse Cookies

Biscuit & Slice Bible p11

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups Plain Flour
  • 1 tsp Bicarbonate of Soda
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 160g Butter (softened)
  • 3/4 cup White Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 cups Chocolate Chips
  • 1 cup Pecans or Walnuts (chopped)

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Sift together flour, bicarbonate of soda and salt
  3. In a separate bowl, cream butter, sugars and vanilla until light and creamy. Beat in eggs
  4. Gradually add flour mixture to butter mixture, mixing until well combined. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts.
  5. Drop teaspoonfuls of dough onto ungreased baking trays, leaving plenty of room for spreading. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
